About

We developed the current prototype of Squarelet in the OER-project edulabs.de.

The funding for edulabs ended in 12/2018, we continue developing the project on a volunteer basis. the code is published with an open licence (AGPL3) and is maintained on github.

We are looking for developers and project partners, to empower a free access to knowledge with self-directed and cooperative learning.

Further planning: github.com/squarelet

Cooperations: info@edulabs.de

Join: github.com/squarelet

Concept

Until 12/2018 we focus on developing a working prototype with basic live-synchronisation and improved design (vuetifyjs)

If a continuation of the project is possible, we plan additional features: Mobile view, more content types, improved synchronisation etc.

Please add feature requests and bug reports directly on github– thanks!
